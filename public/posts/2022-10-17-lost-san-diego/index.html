Lost San Diego

San Diego, like all cities, has been built and re-built over the years. The Save Our Heritage Organisation (SOHO) tries to keep some of the more interesting bits of architecture around for all to enjoy, but some do get lost to history.

Normal School blew me away. I’d seen the annex driving between North Park and Hillcrest, but I did’nt realize it was the locker room to this grand building that has since been torn down in favor of trailers.
